About the Demoracy Idealogy, also a "good example of Demoracy"

They have some several kinds of governments

  1. Conservatism: Considering rapid changes to society to be potentially harmful, conservatism espouses tradition and moderate reforms, sometimes advocating a return to old moral values.
  2. Liberalism: With a long philosophical tradition of valuing personal liberty, liberalism views safeguarding these rights as the primary function of the state.
  3. Socialism: Striving for equality and a socialist state by reformist means, democratic socialism aims to abolish or mitigate the effects of capitalism with public mandate.

Provide some benefits and drawbacks

  1. Time to justify war: -80%
  2. Can occupy territory owned by neutral countries
  3. Can send volunteers
  4. Civillian intelligence to others: +15%
